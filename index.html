Biography

Hi there! Welcome to my personal pages!

I am Chuhao Zhou, a first-year Ph.D. student at the Nanyang Technological University, affiliated with the remarkable MARS Lab @ NTU. I am honoured to work under the supervision of Prof. Jianfei Yang.

Before joining MARS Lab, I completed my B.E. and M.S. degrees at Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen, where I worked at the BRC Lab under the supervision of Prof. Guangming Lu and Prof. Jinxing Li.

My research interests currently lie in Mutlimodal AI and Robotics Perception, especially the multimodal perception and control for robots.
